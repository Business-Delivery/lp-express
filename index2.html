Once Express Checkout is enabled, you can use it to skip directly to the "Review Order" page. Items will be shipped to the default shipping address and charged to the payment method saved to your account.

How to enable Express Checkout

Express Checkout is available once all of these conditions are set-up on your account:

  • 1. Select a default Billing Address on the Address Bookpage.
  • 2. While on the Address Book page, also select a default Shipping Address
  • 3. Under Payment Methods, a stored payment method is set
