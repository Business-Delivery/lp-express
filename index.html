How to enable Express Checkout

Express Checkout is available to the user if all of these conditions are met:

  •   · Under Address Book, a default billing address is on their account
  •   · Also under Address Book, a default shipping address is on their account
  •   · Under Payment Methods, a default payment method is on their account

Once Express Checkout is enabled you can use it to skip directly to the "Review Order" page. Items will be shipped to the default shipping address and charged to the payment method saved to your account.
