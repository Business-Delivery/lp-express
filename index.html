How to enable Express Checkout

  • · Go to your My Account page on CostcoBusinessDelviery.com.
  • · Under Account details enter you Costco Membership number and verify your membership.
  • · Under Address Book, set up a default billing address.
  • · Also under Address Book, set up a default shipping address.
  • · Under Payment Methods, set up a stored payment method

Once Express Checkout is enabled you can use it to skip directly to the "Review Order" page. Items will be shipped to the default shipping address and charged to the payment method saved to your account.
